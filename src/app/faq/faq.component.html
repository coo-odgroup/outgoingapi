What is an e-ticket?

An e–ticket, also known as an electronic ticket, is an electronic document that contains the details of your bus ticket, like a unique confirmation number issued by the bus operator for the booking you have made, along with other details like your departure date, time, boarding point,Conductor number and so on. It is important to carry a print-out of the e-ticket along with a valid photo-id proof at the time of bus departure.

What are bus types?

Bus types indicate the type of bus it is. For example, the standard bus types are Volvo AC buses, AC buses, Non AC buses, Mercedes buses, Non Mercedes buses, Seater buses, Sleeper buses, etc.
Fares vary between AC and non AC seats/berths as well as sleeper or seater options. AC seats/berths are usually more expensive than non AC options. Sleeper berths are usually more expensive than seater options. Same for Valvo cases.
When choosing your seat or sleeper berth on the seat map, please give attention to the type of seat or sleeper berth you are booking, especially in buses which provide both the options.

What are boarding points?

Boarding points are locations within the city from where you can board your bus. In large cities like Bhubaneswar, Sambalpur, etc. there may be more than one boarding point for the same bus. You can choose the boarding point most convenient to you and board the bus from there.(Example- Bhubaneswar Boarding point (Khandagiri,Hotel crown,Jayadev Vihar, Achary Vihar,Satsang Vihar,Palasuni).

Why are different seats priced differently?

The price varies as per the seat type. Some buses have both seats as well as sleeper berths in the same bus, and some buses also have both AC and non AC sections. As an approximate guide, seater seats are priced less than sleeper berths and non AC section is priced less than the AC section. For example, an AC sleeper berth will have a higher fare than a non AC sleeper berth in the same bus. Similarly, a sleeper berth will have a higher fare than a seater in the same bus.

I am travelling with a child. Do I need to purchase a bus ticket for the child?

There will be no charges for children below 2 years of age. They will not be given a seat and will have to travel on an adult's lap/seat. For children over 2 years of age, a full ticket will need to be purchased.

Why does my booking fail?

There could be multiple reasons as to why a booking may fail. If you take too long to make your payment, the seats you selected may no longer be available. In such cases it is advisable to select some other seats and try again. There might also be momentary communication issues at the bus operator end. The departure time shown with the search result is from which boarding point?

The departure time shown along with the search results is the time at which the bus will depart from the first boarding point. Departure times from subsequent boarding points will be after this time. The exact departure time from a boarding point will be mentioned along with the boarding point name, when you choose your boarding point.

How do I find out which are seater seats and which are sleeper berths in the seat layout?

Usually a seat is represented by a square cell while a sleeper berth is rectangular in shape (bigger than a seat cell). The blueprint of the bus which gets displayed on clicking the "View" button will also be carrying details about what seat type it is.
